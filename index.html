Welcome

I am Jiaxuan Li 李嘉轩 (pronounced as Lǐ Jiā Xuān ). I am currently a senior undergraduate student in Department of Astronomy, Peking University.

My research interests focus on a variety of astrophysics and physics. My current research explores the formation and evolution of galaxies in our Universe. I'm also interested in cosmology, sky surveys and supernovae.

Other than astrophysics, I'm interested in statistical physics and nonlinear physics. I studied Faraday pattern on the surface of non-newton fluid using novel data processing approach. More things can be found in Research .

In my homepage, you can find more things than my research. I'm a big fan of any kinds of music, from Indian traditional sidar and sitar to Chopin and Rachmaninoff. I am an amateur astronomer since I was in sixth grade. I took photos for a variety of objects in the sky. Check some of my collection here: Art!

I was born in Dingxi, Gansu, which is a small city in northwest China. Sichuan dishes often appear in my meal list. Check my dishes in Cooking .
